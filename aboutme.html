About Me

Hey! I'm Mr. Pollution, the soul behind the SongBook project hosted on Hacker404.site.

My Passion for Music

Music is more than just sound to me — it's emotion, energy, and expression. From lyrical beauty to heart-touching melodies, I’ve always been obsessed with the magic music creates in our lives.

How We Started SongBook

SongBook began as a small idea rooted in my deep love for music. While working on Hacker404.site for cybersecurity learning, I felt something was missing — a place to celebrate the music I love. That’s when I decided to blend my tech skills with my passion and create SongBook.

What started as a simple lyrics page slowly grew into a full space for music lovers. From jotting down favorite lyrics to curating artist tributes, SongBook became my way of expressing the soul behind the sound.

About Hacker404.site

Hacker404 started as a cyber learning and ethical hacking website. But with my love for music, I launched SongBook, a space where lyrics, trends, and artist tributes come together in a clean, simple layout that anyone can enjoy.

Why You See Ads

To keep this site alive and growing, we display a few minimal ads. These help cover hosting and maintenance costs without disturbing your experience. We promise not to overload you — just enough to support what we love doing!
